Face Recall

Practice remembering names and faces.

Study the faces, names, and facts below. Take your time!

Select the correct name for each person.

Meet & Repeat

  • Hear it: Make sure you clearly hear the name. If not, ask them to repeat it.
  • Say it: Use their name immediately in conversation. "It's nice to meet you, Sarah."
  • Spell it: If it's a unique name, ask how it's spelled to create a mental text image in your mind.

The Association Technique

  • Features: Pick a distinct facial feature to help memorize the person.
  • Link the name: Link their name to something about their appearance or job. Make it as silly as possible so it's easier to remember.

Examples using our Practice Faces:

  • Sofia Grant (Doctor): Imagine her sitting on a Sofa (Sofia) while Granting (Grant) health wishes to her patients.
  • Carlos Mendez (Park Ranger): Picture him driving a Car (Carlos) through the forest, stopping to Mend (Mendez) broken tree branches.
  • Xiao Zhan (Actor): Think about some Xiaolongbao (Xiao) for his last name. Ooh you want some soup dumplings so bad rn.
